# Core Family Foundation

## Product Identity

- App Name: Core Family
- Mission Tagline: A Family Reformation to the World
- Primary Goal:
  - Strengthen marriages
  - Reduce stress and divorce rates, with a long-term target of reducing divorce by 60%
  - Improve parenting skills
  - Provide accessible global counseling and training

## Product Positioning

- Platform Type: Global family and marriage counseling, education, and training platform
- Public Positioning Model: Principle-based, faith-friendly, non-denominational in public language
- Internal Foundation: Faith-rooted family-strengthening philosophy
- Strategic Positioning Rule:
  - Public language should emphasize family strengthening, responsibility, commitment, parenting support, and measurable social impact
  - Faith-integrated content can be optional, but the public platform should remain broadly accessible for global scale and lower regulatory friction

## Mission Statement

To strengthen marriages, empower parents, and build stable families worldwide by providing structured education, verified counseling, and faith-centered relationship development rooted in divine principles.

## Expanded Mission Statement

We exist to promote the sanctity of marriage, the sacred responsibility of parenthood, and the strengthening of families as the fundamental unit of society. Through accessible education, professional counseling, and structured family programs, we aim to help individuals build successful marriages and raise children in love, righteousness, and responsibility.

## Vision Statement

To become the leading global digital platform for marriage and family strengthening, helping reduce divorce rates, promote responsible parenthood, and foster stable societies built upon strong families.

## Long-Term Vision Metrics

- Reduce divorce rates in participating communities by 60%
- Provide affordable family education to over 1 million households globally
- Establish a worldwide network of certified family instructors and counselors

## Core Beliefs

- Marriage between a man and a woman is divinely ordained
- The family is the fundamental unit of society
- Parenthood is a sacred responsibility
- Children deserve love, stability, and moral guidance
- Fathers and mothers are equal partners with distinct responsibilities
- Faith-based principles produce stronger marriages
- Stable families create stable nations

## Core Values

- Sanctity of Marriage: Honor and promote lawful, faithful marriage
- Accountability: Parents and spouses are accountable for their responsibilities
- Compassion and Support: Support individuals in difficult family circumstances
- Education Before Crisis: Emphasize prevention over repair
- Professional Integrity: All counselors and instructors must be verified
- Cultural Respect: Adapt content globally while maintaining moral principles

## Target Users

### General Users (Clients)

Users sign up to receive materials, programs, or counseling. Content should be tailored based on selected category:

- Single Individual
- Married Man or Woman
- Parent
- Family
- Youth or Young Adult

### Certified Instructors

Example: Strengthening Families Program trainers and similar educators.

Instructor requirements:

- Upload certificate
- Upload qualifications
- Maintain profile with bio, training materials, videos, lessons, and program duration
- Option to host live programs

Instructor program model:

- Users enroll in a structured program
- Users pay a small access fee, typically $3 to $5
- Users join weekly sessions through Zoom or in-app video
- Users access quizzes, surveys, and downloadable materials

### Professional Counselors and Therapists

Counselor requirements:

- Upload license or certificate
- Upload qualifications
- Create professional profile
- Set their own session fee

Counselor platform model:

- Annual counselor registration fee: $5, subject to change
- Annual video upload fee: $5, subject to change
- Counselors choose session rates based on country and experience
- Platform takes 5% commission on counseling sessions
- Clients can choose a counselor directly or use an "I need help" flow for counselor recommendation
